#Our F1 Companies
The companies that manufacture the cars in Formula 1
The companies that manufacture the cars in Formula 1 are called "constructors." For the 2024 season, the main F1 constructors are: Red Bull Racing Honda Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team Scuderia Ferrari McLaren F1 Team Aston Martin Aramco Cognizant F1 Team Alpine F1 Team (Renault) Williams Racing Alfa Romeo F1 Team Stake (Sauber) Haas F1 Team AlphaTauri (formerly Toro Rosso, owned by Red Bull) Each of these teams designs and builds their own chassis, while engine suppliers like Mercedes, Honda, Ferrari, and Renault provide the power units.

Oscar Piastri won the latest F1 race at the 2024 Azerbaijan Grand Prix. Starting from second, he overtook Charles Leclerc on lap 20 and managed to hold onto the lead despite intense pressure. Leclerc finished second, while George Russell secured third place after a dramatic collision between Sergio Perez and Carlos Sainz in the final laps, which took them out of the race​( PlanetF1 )​( Sporting News ). This was Piastri's second win of the season, and his performance, along with teammate Lando Norris' strong drive, helped McLaren overtake Red Bull in the constructors' standings​( Formula 1® - The Official F1® Website ).
